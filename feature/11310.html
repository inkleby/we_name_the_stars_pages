Mee W (Moon)

Satellite Feature of Mee

Named after Arthur Butler Phillips Mee - Scottish astronomer (1860-1926).

Mee is a lunar crater that is located in the southwestern part 's near side. Overlying the northwestern rim and intruding one-third the distance across the interior floor is Hainzel, a merged triple-crater formation. To the south is the highly elongated crater Schiller. Mee is 132 kilometers in diameter and 2.7 kilometers deep. (wikipedia entry)
