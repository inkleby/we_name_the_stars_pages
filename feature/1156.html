Charlois Regio (Eros)

Named after Auguste Honore Pierre Regio - French astronomer, independent discoverer of Eros (1864-1910).

A regio is a large area marked by reflectivity or color distinctions from adjacent areas, or a broad geographic region. On Eros they are named after discoverers of Eros.
