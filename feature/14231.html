Ghanim (Enceladus)

Son of Ayyub, brother of Fitnah in the "Tale of Ghanim Bin Ayyub, the Distraught, the Thrall O’Love"

Ghanim is an impact crater in the northern hemisphere of Saturn's moon Enceladus. The topography of the impact crater appears very subdued, suggesting that the crater has undergone significant viscous relaxation since its formation. Tectonics has also affected this crater, by influencing the final, polygonal shape of the crater as well disrupting the southeastern and northwestern margins of the crater following its formation. (wikipedia entry)

A crater is a circular depression likely created by an impact event. On Enceladus they are named after people and places from Burton's Arabian Nights.
