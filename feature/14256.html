Mosul Sulci (Enceladus)

Named after the City where Isaac was pulled up in a basket to a mansion and four damsels in the tale “Isaac of Mosul.”

A sulcus is an are subparallel furrows and ridges. On Enceladus they are named after people and places from Burton's Arabian Nights.
