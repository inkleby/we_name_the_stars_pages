Evander (Dione)

Son of Mercury by Carmentis, ally of Aeneas against the Latins, mythical king of Arcadia, founded and ruled Pallanteum, built on the future site of Rome.

A crater is a circular depression likely created by an impact event. On Dione they are named after people and places from Virgil's Aeneid.
