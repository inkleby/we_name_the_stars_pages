Drepanum Chasma (Dione)

Named after the Coastal town of Sicily where Aeneas found a safe harbor during a raging storm, and where Anchises died.

A chasma is a deep, elongated, steep-sided depression. On Dione they are named after people and places from Virgil's Aeneid.
