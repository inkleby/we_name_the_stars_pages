Harahvaiti Fossa (Rhea)

Beautiful land created by Persian god Ahura Mazda (Ormazd); modern Kandahar, Afghanistan.

A fossa is a long, narrow, shallow depression. On Rhea they are named after people and places from creation myths (with Asian emphasis).
