Handir Colles (Titan)

Son of Haldir, a ruler of the fictional Folk of Haleth; character from Middle-earth, the fictional setting in fantasy novels by J.R.R. Tolkien.

A collis is a collection of small hills or knobs. On Titan they are named after characters from Middle-earth, the fictional setting in fantasy novels by English author J.R.R. Tolkien (1892-1973).
