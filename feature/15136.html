Terror Rupes (Mercury)

Named after English Rupes - ship of discovery that took part in the Ross Antarctic expedition (1839-1843) and Franklin Arctic expedition (1845).

A rupes is a scarp. On Mercury they are named after ships of discovery or scientific expeditions.
