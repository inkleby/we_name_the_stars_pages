Royllo Insula (Titan)

Named after the Island in Atlantic Ocean (15th Century maps) on verge of the unknown, near Antilla and St. Brandan.

A insula is an island (islands), an isolated land area (or group of such areas) surrounded by, or nearly surrounded by, a liquid area (sea or lake). On Titan they are named after islands from legends and myths.
