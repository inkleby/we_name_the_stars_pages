Dsonkwa Regio (Venus)

Kwakiutl (NW Coast) forest giantess.

A regio is a large area marked by reflectivity or color distinctions from adjacent areas, or a broad geographic region. On Venus they are named after giantesses and Titanesses (also two Greek alphanumeric).
