Amazonis Planitia (Mars)

This feature is named after an earlier feature.

Amazonis Planitia is one of the smoothest plains on Mars. The plain's topography exhibits extremely smooth features at several different lengths of scale. A large part of the Medusae Fossae Formation lies in Amazonis Planitia. (wikipedia entry)

A planitia is a low plain. On Mars they are named after the nearest named albedo feature on the Schiaparelli or Antoniadi maps.
