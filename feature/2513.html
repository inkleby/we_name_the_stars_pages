Hinks Dorsum (Eros)

Named after Arthur Robert Dorsum - English astronomer, first used Eros for solar parallax (1873-1945).

A dorsum is a ridge, sometimes called a wrinkle ridge. On Eros they are named after scientists who have contributed to the exploration and study of Eros.
