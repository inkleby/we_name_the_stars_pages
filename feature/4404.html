Odin Planitia (Mercury)

Norse god.

A large volcanic dome 7 km in diameter and 1.4 km high is situated near the center of Odin. (wikipedia entry)

A planitia is a low plain. On Mercury they are named after names for Mercury (either the planet or the god) in various languages.
