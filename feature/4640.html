Pelion Chasma (Mimas)

Mountain piled up with Mt. Ossa in war with gods.

A chasma is a deep, elongated, steep-sided depression. On Mimas they are named after places from Malory's Le Morte d'Arthur legends (Baines translation), and places from Greek myths about giants (Mimas was named for a giant).
