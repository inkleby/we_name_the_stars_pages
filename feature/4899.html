Quetzalpetlatl Corona (Venus)

Aztec fertility goddess.

Quetzalpetlatl Corona is a corona in Lada Terra on the planet Venus. It has a diameter of 780 kilometers, and is the 4th largest corona on Venus. It lies in part on the Lada Rise, with part of the corona intersecting the Ammavuro-Quetzalpetlatl belt in the northwestern region of Lada Terra. (wikipedia entry)

A corona is an oval feature. On Venus they are named after fertility and earth goddesses.
