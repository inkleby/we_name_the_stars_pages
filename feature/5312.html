Santa María Rupes (Mercury)

Spanish; Columbus' flagship, first voyage to America, 1492.

Santa María Rupes is an escarpment on Mercury. The term "rupes" is the Latin word for cliff. (wikipedia entry)

A rupes is a scarp. On Mercury they are named after ships of discovery or scientific expeditions.
