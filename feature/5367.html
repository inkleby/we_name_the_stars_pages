Schiaparelli Dorsum (Mercury)

Named after Giovanni V. Dorsum - Italian astronomer (1835-1910).

A dorsum is a ridge, sometimes called a wrinkle ridge. On Mercury they are named after deceased scientists who have contributed to the study of Mercury.
