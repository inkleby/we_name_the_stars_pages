Tindr (Callisto)

Norse; Ottar's ancestor.

Tindr is a crater on Jupiter's moon Callisto. This is an example of a central pit impact crater. (wikipedia entry)

A crater is a circular depression likely created by an impact event. On Callisto they are named after characters from myths and folktales of cultures of the Far North.
