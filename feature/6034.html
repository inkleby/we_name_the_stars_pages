Titian (Mercury)

Named after Tiziano Vecellio Titian - Italian Renaissance painter (c. 1488/90-1576).

Titian is an impact crater on the planet Mercury. In an enhanced color image, viewed from the MESSENGER spacecraft, the smooth floor is a brighter orange color than the surrounding area, likely due to being filled with volcanic material.
