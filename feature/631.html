Bassorah Fossa (Enceladus)

Named after the Town from which Sindbad embarked on his 3rd voyage.

Bassorah Fossa is a trough south of Ali Baba crater on Saturn's moon Enceladus. Bassorah Fossa was first seen in Voyager 2 images. (wikipedia entry)

A fossa is a long, narrow, shallow depression. On Enceladus they are named after people and places from Burton's Arabian Nights.
