Victoria Rupes (Mercury)

Spanish; Magellan's and Del Cano's ship, first voyage around the world.

Victoria Rupes is an escarpment in the Victoria quadrangle of Mercury. (wikipedia entry)

A rupes is a scarp. On Mercury they are named after ships of discovery or scientific expeditions.
