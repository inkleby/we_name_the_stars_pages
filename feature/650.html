Ba'het Corona (Venus)

Egyptian goddess of wealth and abundance.

Bahet Corona is a feature on Venus about 230 kilometres across. It is adjacent to Onatah Corona. Both features are surrounded by a ring of ridges and troughs, which in places cut more radially-oriented fractures. The centers of the features also contain radial fractures as well as volcanic domes and flows. (wikipedia entry)

A corona is an oval feature. On Venus they are named after fertility and earth goddesses.
