Quivira (Titan)

Named after the Legendary city in the American Southwest; site of a fabulous treasure sought by Coronado and other explorers.

A albedo feature is an area which shows a contrast in brightness or darkness (albedo) with adjacent areas. On Titan they are named after sacred or enchanted places, paradise, or celestial realms from legends, myths, stories, and poems of cultures from around the world.
