Birt (Moon)

Named after William R. Birt - British selenographer (1804-1881).

Birt is a lunar impact crater located in the eastern half of the Mare Nubium and west of the Rupes Recta. (wikipedia entry)

A crater is a circular depression likely created by an impact event. On the Moon they are named after deceased scientists, polar explorers, astronauts or cosmonauts.
