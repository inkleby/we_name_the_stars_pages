Callisto

Upsystem: Ganymede, Downsystem: Janus

Callisto is tidally locked to Jupiter - rotating at the same rate as it orbits. On one side you would never see Jupiter, on the other it would hang in the same place in the sky while the universe revolves around it.

Callisto has 153 named features from 20 nations/peoples.
