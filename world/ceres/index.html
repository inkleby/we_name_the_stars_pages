Ceres

Upsystem: Gaspra, Downsystem: Steins

Ceres is a dwarf planet and the largest object in the asteroid belt - accounting for 1/3 of its entire mass.

Discovered at the start of the 19th Century, like Pluto it spent half a century being considered a planet before other similar objects being discovered led to its current classification.

Ceres has 81 named features from 54 nations/peoples.
