Europa

Upsystem: Io, Downsystem: Ganymede

Europa is covered by a smooth crust of ice around 10-30 km thick. It is believed that the same process that gives Io its volcanism warms the interior of Europa and supports a water ocean under the ice - this ocean's volume would be twice the size of Earth's.
