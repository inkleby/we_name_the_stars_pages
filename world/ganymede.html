Ganymede

Upsystem: Europa, Downsystem: Callisto

Ganymede is the largest moon of Jupiter - it is larger than Mercury but less than half as heavy.

It is the largest object in the solar system not to have a substantial atmosphere.

Ganymede has 194 named features from 19 nations/peoples.
