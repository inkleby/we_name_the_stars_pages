Mercury

Downsystem: Venus

Mercury is the closest planet to the sun.

Names given to Mercury are often associated with messengers or speed.

While it travels fast around the sun, it rotates so slowly you would only need to travel at 11 kmph on the equator to stay in the night-side of the planet.

Mercury has 474 named features from 88 nations/peoples.
