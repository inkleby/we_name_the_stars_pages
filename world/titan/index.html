Titan

Upsystem: Rhea, Downsystem: Hyperion

Titan is a moon of Saturn. Its naming conventions are a blow against the classicist bloc that named the rest of the solar system.

"All these worlds are yours, except Titan. Attempt no namings there." - Fantasy nerds at the IAU (probably).

Titan has 194 named features from 77 nations/peoples.
