Triton

Upsystem: Proteus, Downsystem: Pluto

Triton is a moon of Neptune.

It is believed to be a captured dwarf planet similar to Pluto that formed elsewhere before eventually falling into orbit around Neptune.

Triton has 61 named features from 33 nations/peoples.
